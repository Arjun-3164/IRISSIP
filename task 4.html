Nambi Narayan

Tribute page



This is a tribute page to Nambi Narayan. S. Nambi Narayanan is an Indian aerospace scientist who worked for the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) and contributed significantly to the Indian space program by developing the Vikas rocket engine. He was also instrumental in developing the cryogenic engine technology for ISRO.. Here are some of their accomplishments:

  • 1.Developed the Vikas rocket engine
  • 2.Developed the cryogenic engine technology
  • 3.Cleared of all charges in the ISRO espionage case

Nambi Narayanan is a true hero of the Indian space program. He has made significant contributions to the development of India's space program and has helped to put India on the map as a leading spacefaring nation.
